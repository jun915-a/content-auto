# Notion Leaks Email Addresses

Notion leaks email addresses of all editors of public pages, raising concerns about user privacy and data security

## 🔑 The Core of This Topic
Notion, a popular note-taking and collaboration platform, has been found to leak the email addresses of all editors of any public page, potentially compromising user privacy.

## ⚡ 5-Second Key Points
- **Point 1**: Email addresses of editors are visible to anyone with the page link
- **Point 2**: This issue affects all public Notion pages
- **Point 3**: Users are advised to review their page settings for privacy

## 📈 Detailed Breakdown
**Element 1**
The leak is a result of Notion's current implementation of public page sharing, where editor email addresses are included in the page's metadata.

**Element 2**
This metadata is accessible to anyone who has the link to the public page, regardless of whether they have a Notion account or not.

> 💡 Insight: This highlights the importance of carefully considering what information is shared publicly, especially in collaborative environments.

## 🎯 Real-World Impact
- Users may receive unwanted emails or spam
- Editors may be targeted by malicious actors
- Notion's reputation could be damaged due to privacy concerns

## ✨ Conclusion
Notion needs to address this issue to protect its users' privacy and maintain trust in its platform. Users should also be cautious about what they share publicly and review their settings regularly.
