# ASML's Dominance in Chip Production

ASML's cutting-edge machines have become the bottleneck in the production of advanced computer chips, affecting industries worldwide.

## 🔑 The Core of This Topic
ASML's machines are used to create the most complex patterns on silicon wafers, a crucial step in the production of computer chips. The company's equipment has become the limiting factor in the industry, with many companies relying on ASML for their production needs.

## 📈 Detailed Breakdown
**Element 1**
ASML's machines use a process called extreme ultraviolet lithography (EUVL) to create patterns on silicon wafers. This process involves projecting a pattern onto the wafer using a beam of extreme ultraviolet light. The resulting pattern is then used to create the desired circuits on the wafer.

**Element 2**
The EUVL process is extremely complex and requires precise control over many variables. ASML's machines are designed to handle this complexity, but the company's equipment has become a bottleneck in the industry. Many companies are struggling to keep up with demand for ASML's machines, leading to delays in chip production.

> 💡 Insight: The dominance of ASML's machines has created a single point of failure in the industry, with many companies relying on the company for their production needs.
