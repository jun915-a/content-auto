## 🔑 The Core of This Topic
The decision to cancel Claude was largely driven by three main factors: token issues, declining quality, and poor support.

## ⚡ 5-Second Key Points
- **Token Issues**: Frequent errors and limitations
- **Declining Quality**: Reduced performance over time
- **Poor Support**: Unresponsive and unhelpful customer service

## 📈 Detailed Breakdown
**Token Issues**
Token issues were a significant problem, causing frustration and disruption for users.

**Declining Quality**
The quality of Claude's services decreased, leading to a poor user experience.

> 💡 Insight: The combination of these issues made it difficult for users to continue with the service.

## 🎯 Real-World Impact
- Increased frustration among users
- Loss of trust in the service
- Exploration of alternative options

## ✨ Conclusion
The cancellation of Claude is a reminder that quality and support are crucial for any service to succeed.
