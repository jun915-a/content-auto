# Your Period Tracking App Has Been Selling Data to Meta

A shocking investigation reveals that many popular period tracking apps are secretly selling user data to Meta, putting women's health and personal information at risk.

Women's health apps have become increasingly popular in recent years, providing users with valuable information about their menstrual cycles. However, a recent investigation has uncovered a disturbing trend: many of these apps are selling user data to Meta, the parent company of Facebook and Instagram.
