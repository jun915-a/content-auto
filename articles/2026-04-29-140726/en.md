# Ghostty Leaving GitHub

Ghostty's departure from GitHub sparks discussion on open-source sustainability and community impact

## 🔑 The Core of This Topic
Ghostty, a significant contributor, is leaving GitHub due to concerns over the platform's direction and open-source sustainability.

## ⚡ 5-Second Key Points
- **Loss of Talent**: Ghostty's departure highlights the risk of losing valuable contributors.
- **Open-Source Concerns**: Questions about the long-term viability of open-source projects on GitHub.
- **Community Impact**: Potential effects on the broader developer community and project collaborations.

## 📈 Detailed Breakdown
**Contributor Burnout**
Ghostty's exit underscores the burnout and frustration some contributors face when their work is not adequately recognized or supported.

**Platform Sustainability**
The departure also raises questions about GitHub's ability to sustain and support open-source projects in the long term.

> 💡 Insight: The health of open-source projects is closely tied to the well-being and support of their contributors.

- Potential decrease in the quality of open-source projects

## ✨ Conclusion
Ghostty's decision to leave GitHub serves as a reminder of the importance of supporting contributors and ensuring the sustainability of open-source platforms.
