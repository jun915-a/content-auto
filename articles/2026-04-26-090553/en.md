# 1-Bit Hokusai's Wave

Discover the stunning 1-bit pixel art recreation of Hokusai's iconic 'The Great Wave off Kanagawa', a masterpiece of Japanese art reimagined in a modern digital format

## The Core of This Topic
The 1-bit pixel art of Hokusai's 'The Great Wave off Kanagawa' is a fascinating recreation of the classic Japanese artwork, reduced to the simplest form of digital art. 
## 5-Second Key Points
- **Point 1**: 1-bit art uses only two colors
- **Point 2**: The original artwork is from the 19th century
- **Point 3**: Pixel art is a modern digital medium
## Detailed Breakdown
**Element 1**
The original 'Great Wave' is a famous Japanese ukiyo-e woodblock print. 
**Element 2**
The 1-bit version maintains the essence of the original, with bold lines and contrasting colors.
> Insight: The simplicity of 1-bit art can still convey the power of the original image.
