## The Core of This Topic
The USB Cheat Sheet provides a comprehensive overview of the Universal Serial Bus, covering its architecture, protocols, and applications. 
## 5-Second Key Points
- **USB Versions**: USB 1.0, 2.0, 3.0, and 3.2
- **Transfer Speeds**: Low, Full, High, and SuperSpeed
- **Connectors**: Type-A, Type-B, Mini, and Micro
## Detailed Breakdown
**USB Architecture**
The USB architecture is based on a host-controller interface, which manages data transfer between devices.
**USB Protocols**
USB protocols define the rules for data transfer, including packet formatting and error handling.
> Insight: Understanding USB protocols is crucial for developing compatible devices.
