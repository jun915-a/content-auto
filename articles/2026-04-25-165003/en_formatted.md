## 🔑 The Core of This Topic
Overthinking and scope creep can lead to project sabotage, affecting deadlines and overall success.

## ⚡ 5-Second Key Points
- **Overthinking**: Leads to analysis paralysis
- **Scope Creep**: Uncontrolled changes in project scope
- **Structural Diffing**: Inefficient comparison of project structures

## 📈 Detailed Breakdown
**Overthinking**
Overthinking can cause delays and reduce productivity. 
**Scope Creep**
Scope creep can lead to project failure by changing the initial goals.

> 💡 Insight: Recognizing these pitfalls is key to avoiding them.

## 🎯 Real-World Impact
- Delays in project completion
- Increased costs due to scope changes
- Reduced team morale

## ✨ Conclusion
Avoiding overthinking, scope creep, and structural diffing is crucial for project success.
