# B-52 Star Tracker

Discover the electromechanical angle computer inside the B-52 bomber's star tracker, a crucial navigation component

## The Core of This Topic
The B-52 star tracker's electromechanical angle computer is a complex system that calculates the bomber's position and orientation. 
## 5-Second Key Points

- **Point 2**: Uses analog computers
- **Point 3**: Enhances bombing accuracy
## Detailed Breakdown
**Element 1**
The star tracker uses a gyro-stabilized platform to maintain its orientation. 
**Element 2**
The analog computer processes data from the star tracker and gyroscopes.
