# Localsend: AirDrop Alternative

Localsend is an open-source cross-platform alternative to AirDrop, allowing easy file sharing between devices

## 🔑 The Core of This Topic
Localsend is a free and open-source platform that enables users to share files between devices effortlessly. It is designed to be a cross-platform alternative to AirDrop, providing a seamless experience across different operating systems.
